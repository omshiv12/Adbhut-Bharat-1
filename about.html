About Us



A customer has to approach various agencies to find details of places and to book tickets. This requires a lot of time and efforts. This also sometimes misguides customers so it becomes tedious.

Our site will allow one to easily access the relevant information and make necessary travel arrangements. Users can decide about places they want to visit by some past experiences of other users as well and make bookings online for travel and accommodation at convenient cost easily from home.

This website will facilitate the users to easily plan their trips to various tourist places in India. Users can select their ideal trips by logging in with their account.

They will be able to know about various travel agencies and will also be able to download E-Guide (if available) of that place. There will be various filters to facilitate users to quickly and easily search for best locations.
